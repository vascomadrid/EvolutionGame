UofSC Haunted Moms Film Series presents:

Pac-man style game based off of Evolution(2015) directed by Lucile Hadžihalilović

How to Play

You are a boy in the upper left corner
1. Move using arrow keys
2. Collect all items to win
3. Avoid getting caught by nurse
4. Eat green pasta to passify nurse, touch passified nurse (glowing yellow) to send her to her lair
